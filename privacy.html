Privacy Policy

What data do we collect?

Data collected automatically

During your visit on our website, we collect your IP address, browser and operating system type.

When you contact us via e-mail or contact form, you provide us with your personal details like name, surname and e-mail address.

How do we process your data?

Under no circumstances will we sell data collected about you to third parties. Data collected automatically can be used to analyze the behavior and origin of users visiting the site or to personalize the content of our site. Data is collected automatically for each user.

Data collected during the correspondence between you and our website will be used only to respond to your inquiry.
In case of the Inspector General for Personal Data Protection inspection your data may be made available to the employees of the Inspectorate in accordance with the Law on Personal Data Protection.

In the event of a breach of the Regulations of our site, violation of the law, or if required by law, we may disclose your information to the judicial authorities.

Cookie files (cookies)

Cookies are very small files that the web page saves in your browser's clipboard on your computer. Thanks to such files, it can remember your individual settings on given page.

Changes to our privacy policy

We reserve the right to change this privacy policy by publishing a new privacy policy on this site.
If you have additional questions regarding privacy protection, please contact us.
