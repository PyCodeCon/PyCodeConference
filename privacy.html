PyCode Conference

PyCode Conference is a conference devoted to the Python language. The organizers are people from the language community.

Privacy Policy

Below you will find the information that the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) law requires us to give you. We act in accordance with the law and the information below is detailed.

What data do we collect?

During your visit on our website, we collect your IP address, browser and operating system type.

When you contact us via e-mail or contact form, you provide us with your personal details like name, surname and e-mail address.

How do we process your data?

The data can be shared with other PyCode Conference organizers and its partners listed on the conference website.

Data we collect is going to be deleted automatically no later than 1 year after you provided it.

Under no circumstances will we sell data collected about you to third parties.

Data collected automatically can be used to analyze the behavior and origin of users visiting the site or to personalize the content of our site. Data is collected automatically for each user.

In case of the Inspector General for Personal Data Protection inspection your data may be made available to the employees of the Inspectorate in accordance with the Law on Personal Data Protection.

In the event of a breach of the regulations of our site, violation of the law, or if required by law, we may disclose your information to the judicial authorities.

Third parties services

We use the listed services to store your data:

  • As the website and e-mail hosting: Thecamels.
  • For data collected using Google Forms: Google Cloud.
  • For registration purposes Eventil.

Why do we collect you data

Data collected during the correspondence between you and our website will be used to respond to your inquiry, registration to the conference and to organize the conference for attendees to the best of our abilities.

Cookie files (cookies)

Cookies are very small files that the web page saves in your browser's clipboard on your computer. Thanks to such files, it can remember your individual settings on given page.

Contact

If you have additional questions regarding privacy protection, please contact us.
